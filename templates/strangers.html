Good practice when talking to strangers!

Of course, an adventure is incomplete without meeting new friends! However, on our journey of building connections with people on the other side of the world, it’s essential to be mindful of the personal information we disclose. Imagine chatting with a stranger on the street, you wouldn’t reveal your address and phone number right? Similarly, we shouldn’t share sensitive information to the new friends we meet online. Here are some important factor to keep in mind when you chat with your new Internet friends:
  • Use nicknames

    By not revealing your real name, you can protect your identity from being exposed to strangers, and lower the chance of being stalked.

  • Be Aware of links and files

    Unless you are totally confident that the file and links are safe, do not download/click on it. Suspicious links and files can make your computer vulnerable to hackers.

  • Keep your secret

    You never know who sits on the other side of the computer, so make sure not to tell them your secret, personal information and other private stuff!

  • Verify their identity

    If your real-world friend sends you something suspicious on the Internet, it’s possible that their account has been hacked! In this case, try to double-check with your friends and make sure that the person you're talking to is actually the person you know.

Although we want to trust that the world is full of friendly faces, it's important to keep in mind that, unfortunately, there are individuals with malicious intentions. By maintaining good practices of caution, awareness, and privacy, we can enjoy our online adventures while prioritizing our safety and security!
